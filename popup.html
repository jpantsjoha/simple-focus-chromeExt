Pomodoro Timer for Deep Focus

A minimalist productivity tool that helps you maintain focus using the proven Pomodoro Technique.

How it Works:

  • 8 Dots = 1 Complete Cycle
  • 4 Focus sessions (25 min each)
  • 3 Short breaks (5 min each)
  • 1 Long break (15 min)

Interactive Features:

  • Progress Bar: Hover to see blue handle, drag to reduce time
  • Dot Editing: Click dots to customize session durations
  • Website Blocking: Automatically blocks distracting sites during focus
